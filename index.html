ABOUT US

Established in 2001 ,multi-tier, floodlit driving range, powered by Toptracer Range technology. Complimented by a practice green and bunker, coffee shop and INDIAN Golf Store. There truly is something for everyone as we also boast two outdoor 18-hole dinosaur themed crazy golf courses.

Adventure Golf

Adventure Golf isn't just about the miniature golf; it is about the whole experience. One of the key differences is the way we layout our holes, we tend to stay away from man-made obstacles placed on the course and instead we use the holes themselves to create dips, dives, whirls and potential pit falls.
